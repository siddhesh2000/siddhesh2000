1]List and explain different PowerBi products?
Ans:
a] Power BI Desktop:
   - It is the main Power BI Application where an Analyst can connect application to multiple data sources and analyze data to create reports to business stakeholder.
   - This product comes with free, pro and premium versions which costs accordingly.
b] Power Bi Service:
  - It is a product where stakeholders can get access to reports created by developers/Analyst to see performance of their business or whatever task assigned to the Team. 
  - Stakeholders can get access to them oce analyst publishes them in the shared workspace.
c] Power Bi Mobile:
  - It is similar to Power BI Service. 
  - Stakeholders can access the reports and dashboards through Mobile/Tablet device and view them. 
  - It is meant only for viewing contents which means users can't create dashboards in this platform.

2]  What limitations of Excel, Microsoft solved by PowerBi ?

Ans:
a] The charts and visualizations are much advanced and interactive in Power BI.

d] The maximum number of rows and columns is limited in Excel. Power BI doesn't limit users on dimension of data.
